About Me

I am a Salesforce Certified Developer with over 2.11 years of progressive experience designing, developing, and optimizing enterprise-grade CRM solutions across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, and Education Cloud environments. Currently serving as a Senior Software Engineer II, I specialize in building scalable Salesforce architectures using Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), and robust REST/SOAP integrations.

Throughout my career, I have worked extensively on asynchronous processing including Batch Apex, Queueable Apex, and Scheduled Jobs to handle bulk data operations efficiently while maintaining strict governor limit compliance. I have hands-on experience implementing structured trigger frameworks, reusable utility classes, and performance-optimized SOQL queries to ensure maintainable and high-performing applications.

In addition to core development, I have contributed to AI-driven automation initiatives using Einstein Prediction Builder and Prompt Builder, enabling intelligent workflows and enhanced decision-making processes within Service Cloud implementations. My work focuses on functionality, system reliability, security best practices, and long-term scalability.

I actively participate in design reviews, mentor junior developers, and follow Agile methodologies including sprint planning, estimation, and structured code reviews. Passionate about continuous learning, I constantly explore emerging technologies such as AI-assisted automation, Agentforce concepts, and advanced integration patterns.
